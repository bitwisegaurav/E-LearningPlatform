Introduction to Computer Graphics and Multimedia

Computer Graphics and Multimedia are two important fields in technology. Computer graphics help create and show images using computers, while multimedia combines different types of media like text, images, audio, and video.

Evolution and Importance

Computer graphics and multimedia have become more important as computers have improved. They're used in many areas, like gaming, art, and education. The internet has made multimedia even more popular by letting people share all kinds of media easily.

Key Concepts

Understanding computer graphics means knowing things like how images are made and how they move. For multimedia, it's about knowing how to mix different kinds of media together, like pictures and sound.

Scope of Study

Studying computer graphics and multimedia covers a lot of topics. We learn about making 2D and 3D images, creating virtual worlds, and even how to make interactive stories using different kinds of media.

Conclusion

Computer graphics and multimedia are important parts of technology. They help us make and share images, videos, and more. By studying them, we can learn how to create all kinds of cool things and understand how they work.
